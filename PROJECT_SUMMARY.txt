📊 SCORING SYSTEM

  BASE SCORE = Difficulty × 100
  TIME BONUS = (300 - Time) / 300 (faster is better)
  EFFICIENCY = 1 - (Moves / 100)
  
  FINAL SCORE = BASE × (0.5 × TIME_BONUS + 0.5 × EFFICIENCY)
  
  AUTO-DIFFICULTY:
  ✅ Score ≥ 80% → Increase difficulty
  ✅ Score ≤ 40% → Decrease difficulty
  ✅ Score 40-80% → Maintain difficulty
